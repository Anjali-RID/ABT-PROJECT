About Anjali Bhardwaj

Born and raised in Bihar, Anjali Bhardwaj discovered her passion for devotional music at a young age. Her journey in the world of Bhojpuri devotional music spans over two decades, during which she has touched countless hearts with her soulful renditions. Known for her melodious voice and deep connection to spiritual music, Anjali has become one of the most respected artists in the Bhojpuri music industry.

Her dedication to preserving and promoting traditional devotional music has earned her a special place in the hearts of millions.
